1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ChatFlow ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our real-time chat application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using ChatFlow, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with these practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up using Google Authentication, we collect:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Your display name from Google
  • Google profile picture (if available)
  • Google account ID (unique identifier)

Chat Content

We collect and store:

  • Messages you send and receive in real-time chats
  • Timestamps of your messages
  • Information about message recipients and chat participants

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Login times and session duration
  • Features you use within the application
  • Technical information (browser type, IP address, device information)
  • Error logs and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Authenticate your identity through Google OAuth
  • Enable real-time messaging using WebSockets
  • Display your profile information to other chat participants
  • Maintain your chat history and preferences

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Optimize application performance

Security and Safety

  • Detect and prevent spam, abuse, or security breaches
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Authentication: For secure login and identity verification
  • Hosting Providers: For application infrastructure and data storage
  • Analytics Services: For understanding usage patterns (anonymized data only)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, such as:

  • Responding to valid legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protecting our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Investigating potential violations of our Terms of Service
  • Preventing illegal activities or security issues

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data Retention

  • Chat messages are stored for the duration of your account
  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Backup copies may be retained for up to 30 days after deletion
  • Some data may be retained longer when required by law

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Secure storage of data at rest
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

WebSocket Security

Our real-time chat features using WebSockets and Django Channels include:

  • Secure WebSocket connections (wss://)
  • Authentication for all real-time connections
  • Message validation and sanitization
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse

6. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your chat history and personal data

Account Management

  • Delete Your Account: You can permanently delete your account through the application settings
  • Revoke Google Access: You can revoke ChatFlow's access to your Google account through Google's security settings
  • Opt-out of Analytics: Contact us to exclude your usage data from analytics

Data Portability

Upon request, we will provide you with a copy of your personal data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Essential Cookies

We use cookies necessary for the application to function:

  • Session cookies for maintaining your login state
  • Authentication tokens for secure access
  • WebSocket connection identifiers

Analytics and Performance

We may use analytics cookies to understand:

  • How users interact with our application
  • Feature usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Performance metrics and error rates

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect application functionality.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • We will notify you through the application or via email for significant changes
  • We will provide a summary of material changes when possible

Your continued use of ChatFlow after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
